  • guhana abeteje umutekano muke
  • kurara irondo
  • kumenyesha abayobozi abanzi bamahoro
  • gukumira ibibazo mumiryango
  • kugira inama abafitanye ibibazo
  • gushyiraho abayobozi b'umutekano
  • kumenya ibiteza umutekano muke n'uburyo bwo kubikumira
  • gukora inama z'itandukanye
  • gushyiraho inteko y'abunzi
  • kutanga inama kukibazo wagize
  • kunga abafitanye amakimbirane
